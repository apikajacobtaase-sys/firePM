About FIREPM

A premium spiritual experience shaped by prayer and purpose.

Fire Prayer Movement is a Christ-centered ministry committed to igniting spiritual revival through passionate worship, fervent prayer, and the teaching of God’s Word. The experience is designed to feel modern, rooted, and deeply human.

Prayer Gatherings Worship Nights Bible Teaching Discipleship

Mission

To cultivate a lifestyle of prayer, proclaim the Gospel, disciple believers, and inspire lives that reflect the love and power of Christ.

Vision

To raise a generation of believers who pursue God passionately and transform communities through prayer and the Gospel.

Impact

Families restored, communities strengthened, and a culture of spiritual hunger renewed through consistent prayer and worship.

Vision

Raising a generation that walks in power and purpose.

We believe prayer is not a ritual but a weapon, a refuge, and a pathway to transformation. Our vision is to see believers live in intimacy with God and release His presence wherever they go.

Mission

Cultivating prayerful, Christ-centered, kingdom-minded lives.

Our mission is to inspire holy living, biblical truth, and spiritual hunger through worship, discipleship, and prayer-centered community.
